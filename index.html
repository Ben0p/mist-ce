Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use it?

The easiest way is to sign up to the Hosted Edition. Then you can immediatelly start adding your clouds and inviting your team members.

Can I install it on-premises?

Yes! You can download and install the Community Edition which is free and open source software. Also, you can purchase a license for the Enterprise Edition.

How much does it cost?

The Hosted Edition provides a limited free tier and then implements a Pay-As-You-Go model depending on usage. The Enterprise Edition is licensed per vcpu. Details in the pricing page.

How secure is it?

Mist.io helps you enhance security by implementing access policies consistently across your entire infrastructure. We use Mist.io internally to manage all our systems since 2013. During this time, we never witnessed any evidence of security breaches on our production infrastructure.
